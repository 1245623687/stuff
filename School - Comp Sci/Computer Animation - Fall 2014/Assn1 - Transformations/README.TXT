To Compile and run: 
	open project file in visual studio and and hit cntrl_shift+b to build, and cntrl + f5 to run

Controls:

	World Space Spinner rotates the cube in world space.
	Click and drag the spinner to update rotation values.

	Local space spinner rotates the cube in the local space of the cube
	Click and drag the spinner to update rotation values.

	Translate x, y, and z arrows update translation in each axis respectively.
	Click and drag the spinner to update translation values.
